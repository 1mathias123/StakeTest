Dark Matter is a decentralised open sorce Div payout platform for DMD ( Dark Matter DApps ) tokens, Each tokens can be staked in Excelerator to earn payout once a day, this pays out is more DMD tokens or TRX all earned from housedge from diffrent dapps, aswell as once leaveing or unstakeing from stake, 20% will be used to feedback into the contract. this will ensure a longterm life for stakers and payouts.

The token price can flux and deflux over time, But we hope to set token price for over 1k, We have plans to ensure ( 15,000,000 Trx ) 1k Trx per token at a min this is known as buyback funds, this funds are only effected if token price is under 1k, and are distrbuted overtime. but we hope token price will easily surpass 2k Trx per overtime.
